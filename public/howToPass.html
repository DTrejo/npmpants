How to get your module tested

  1. Make sure sure your package.json has a scripts.test command, like so: 
    "scripts": {
  "test": "nodeunit test.js"
}
  2. Make sure your test suite is one of expresso, jasmine, nodeunit, tap, vows, or whiskey
  3. OR make sure that your test suite returns TAP protocol. If you do none of the above, our test runner will make guesses, and most likely label your tests as failed (sorry!).
